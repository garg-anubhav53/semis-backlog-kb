## 3. Lessons for Pipeline

**(a) The scouting miss.** Both Riber (€60M → €250M) and SEMCO Technologies (IPO €154M Jul-2025 → €523M) were catchable pre-run. Neither surfaced because the screen scans *current* caps, not *entry* caps. **Fix: a graduation-cohort tripwire** — a standing scan of Euronext Growth / small-EU-venue semiconductor-adjacent names by *12-month-trailing* cap that flags anything crossing INTO the band from below, before it exits above.

**(b) Universe exhaustion.** Three-plus consecutive empty runs is not bad luck — the intersection (semi supply chain × sub-$150M × <2 analysts × <$2M volume) is mined out. The last three survivors (TRT, KRMKF, RBERP) all cleared only via venue/GICS gaps, and RBERP cleared *nothing* (cap breach). The methodology is fishing a fished-out pond.

**(c) Pivot — specific next action.** Do not re-run the exhausted gate a 4th time. Instead: (i) **systematic scan of all Euronext Growth IPOs 2021–2025** in semiconductor-adjacent SIC/GICS; (ii) **TSX-V ceramics/quartz/specialty-metals screen** for WFE consumables; (iii) **one controlled cap-band widen to $150–400M** to recapture graduated names (SEMCO, Riber) uncovered by US analysts. Highest-EV move: (i) + (iii).
