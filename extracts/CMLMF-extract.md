## 6. Sole-Source / Qualification / Moat Language

From GNSS contract RNS (July/August 2025):
> "CML's ability to meet stringent manufacturing requirements and provide long-term supply chain resilience are key attributes [that secured the multi-year commitment]."

> "[CML will deliver] advanced design services and product supply [under] a 12-year design and supply agreement with a prominent global manufacturer of industrial GNSS equipment."

> "The initial two-to-three-year design phase is well underway and will pave the way for substantial product sales."

Not explicitly "sole-source" but a 12-year design-and-supply agreement implies functional lock-in: once CML's RF IC is designed into the GNSS platform, customer re-qualification of a competitor would require restarting a multi-year design cycle.
