## SUPPLEMENTAL DATA (from secondary research)

### Siemens Healthineers Contract (exact terms)
"The agreements are worth **$37.5 million (£29.5 million) over four years**, covering licensing, technical support, and the supply of detector materials used in medical scans. Kromek will provide its CZT-based detector tiles to Siemens for the duration over four years, with a one year extension at Siemens Healthineers' discretion."
"Kromek received the first **$25.0 million payment** under its Enablement Agreement with Siemens Healthineers, with additional aggregate payments of **$12.5 million payable over the next four years**. Under the deal, Kromek will transfer **15 of its existing 174 furnaces** used for producing detector materials and provide Siemens Healthineers with expertise and intellectual property for manufacturing."

**Revenue recognition (milestone-based):** £16.5m recognized FY2025; $11.7m guided FY2026; $2.5m expected FY2027.

### UK Government Framework (qualification moat)
"Kromek is pre-qualified under three categories of the government's procurement framework. These categories cover handheld, wearable, and large volume static radiation detectors. Over the four-year term of the Framework, Kromek's pre-qualified categories have a combined maximum procurement value of **£84 million**."
"As one of a limited number of pre-approved suppliers, the company is 'uniquely positioned to support the Home Office's mission to strengthen national security.'"

### US DoD IDIQ Framework
Kromek pre-qualified under US DoD Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Joint Enterprise framework for CBRN detection — **$8.2 billion allocated over 10 years**.

### CZT Supply Monopoly Claim
"Kromek is uniquely positioned as the **only independent commercial-scale producer of CZT globally**."

### Moat Deterioration (caveat)
"In 2025, three other companies, including Canon, came out with either soft product launch or really new product introduction in the market with CZT in their CT systems, photon counting CT systems. The results suggest that Kromek is working with everybody at the moment who does not have access to an internal supply chain, so like Siemens Healthineers and CT does have."
*(The Siemens enablement deal transfers furnaces and IP — Siemens is building internal CZT supply.)*

### Specific Contract Awards
