## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW AND BACKLOG DEFINITION

PVA TePla AG (XETRA: TPE) manufactures:
- **Metrology segment:** Acoustic microscopy and inspection systems for semiconductor wafer and package inspection (SAM — Scanning Acoustic Microscopy)
- **Material Solutions segment:** SiC crystal growing systems (Lely method), vacuum brazing systems, vacuum sintering systems, and sapphire crystal growing

Products serve: semiconductor manufacturers (SiC wafers for power devices/EVs), compound semiconductor fabs, and automotive/electronics inspection end-markets.

**Backlog/Order Intake Definition:** 
PVA TePla reports "order intake" (Auftragseingang in German) as new customer orders received in the period. The "order backlog" (Auftragsbestand) represents unfilled orders carried forward.
